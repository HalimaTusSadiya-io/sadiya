About Me

Hello! I'm Halima Tus Sadiya, a passionate undergraduate student majoring in Communication for Development at the University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB). My academic journey has been shaped by a deep curiosity for media studies, development communication, and the power of storytelling to drive meaningful social change.
